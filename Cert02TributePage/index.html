Mr. Whiskers

The cat who provided a billion smiles

  1. Born on the 4th of July in Abox, Florida.
  2. Shortly after birth he was unceremoniously rendered sterile per local custom.
  3. Took up the odd hobby of stalking and killing small vertebrates and placing the remains on the front porch of the family home.
  4. Went through a rebelious phase refusing to spend more than the bare minimum of time on the floor, rather preferring the top of tall furniture and curtain rods.
  5. His family discovered cat towers.
  6. Spent the bulk of the year isolated per Dr. Fauci's orders.
  7. Went missing. Cause unknown. A canine of interest, Bully age 5, was investigated. Bully's family refused to allow excavation of a crudely dug and covered hole visible in their backyard.
